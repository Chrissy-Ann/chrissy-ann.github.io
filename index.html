About Me

Hi! I'm Christina Hollands, and I am a student at Algonquin College in the Web Development and Internet Applications program. I have a passion for full-stack web development, the creativity of front end design and the critical thinking and problem solving required for back end development. I love fixing broken code, improving upon it, and the feeling of success that comes after persistent debugging, when I have finally found a solution. In full-stack development I have found a way to satisfy both my love of art and creativity, and my fondness of problem solving and puzzles.
